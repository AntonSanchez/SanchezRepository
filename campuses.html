UM BANSALAN

About UM Bansalan
UM Bansalan College is a campus of the University of Mindanao (UM), located at the center of the Municipality of Bansalan, Davao del Sur. Founded in 1962 under the name Mindanao Colleges, it began with 25 high school students in a two-room building and a faculty of five led by the late Mr. Sofronio Bonjoc. After two years, Mr. Roldan L. Valle succeeded him. Through his vision and commitment, the original wooden structure was expanded from two rooms to seven, eventually growing into a nine-room building by 1982.

UM DIGOS

About UM Digos
UM Digos College has come a long way since its inception in 1949 as the first branch of the University of Mindanao, then as the Mindanao Colleges. It started with the offering of high school and 2-year programs such as Associate in Arts and Associate in Commercial Science. Later on in the early 1960s, degree programs, namely; Bachelor of Arts, Bachelor of Science in Commerce, Bachelor of Secondary Education and Bachelor of Elementary Education were offered.

UM PANABO

UM Panabo College
University of Mindanao Panabo College (UM Panabo College) is a private, higher education institution in Panabo City, Davao del Norte. It was established in 1951 and is one of the schools in the University of Mindanao System.

UM TAGUM

About UM Tagum
UM Tagum College is a prominent educational institution located in Tagum City, Davao del Norte, Philippines. As a part of the University of Mindanao system, it offers a wide range of undergraduate and graduate programs designed to cater to the diverse educational needs of its students. Known for its commitment to academic excellence, UM Tagum College provides a supportive and dynamic learning environment, fostering intellectual growth, innovation, and community engagement. With state-of-the-art facilities and a dedicated faculty, the college continues to contribute significantly to the educational and professional development of its students, preparing them for successful careers and meaningful contributions to society.
